📍 6, Il-Fraxxnu · Self check-in via keybox inside the gate
🤚 Step 2 — reach through the gate opening to release the inner latch
1
Find the entrance
Look for Villa Ixoria Unit 1 at 6, Il-Fraxxnu, St Julian's. You'll see a white gate with the name "IXORIA" on the wall.
2
Open the gate
The small pedestrian gate is unlocked from the inside. Reach your hand through the opening in the gate to release the inner latch (see photo).
3
Open the keybox
Once inside, you'll find a black Smiths keybox marked with the number "1". Use the code 6745 to open it and take out the key labeled "Unit 1".
4
You're in!
Use the key to unlock the door of Unit 1 (ground level). Welcome to Villa Ixoria Unit 1! Please remember to put the key back in the keybox on departure. If anything is unclear or you need help, message Martina via WhatsApp at any time.

House Rules
  • 🚭
    No smoking indoors — €250 fee applies
    Smoking is only permitted on balconies or outdoor areas. Please close windows and doors when smoking outside.
  • 🌿
    Help us keep it green
    Switch off lights and air conditioning when you head out — even for a short while.
  • 🤫
    Noise & neighbours
    Please keep noise low between 11:00 PM and 8:00 AM.
  • 🎉
    No parties or events — €250 fee applies
    Parties and events of any kind are not permitted in the apartment or building.
  • 🗑️
    Waste collection
    Please take garbage bags out only during the designated collection windows: morning 08:00–08:30, evening 18:00–18:30. Do not leave garbage in corridors or outside the apartment.
  • 🧺
    Towels — fee based on damage
    Please do not heavily stain towels. Replacement costs will apply if towels are damaged beyond normal use.
  • 🐾
    No pets
    Pets are not permitted. This is a building regulation that applies to all residents and guests.
Before you settle in
Good to Know
🏊 Private pool
Unit 1 has access to the ground-level pool. Please follow basic safety guidelines — no glass near the water, supervise children at all times.
🌿 Private garden
Enjoy the outdoor space. Please respect the plants and keep the area tidy.
🏠 Private apartment
This is a private apartment, not a hotel. Daily cleaning and towel changes are not included.
🧹 Additional cleaning & towels
Extra cleaning or towel changes can be requested separately, subject to availability and extra charges.
🔌 UK plug sockets
Malta uses three-pin UK-style plugs. Bring a universal adapter if needed.
🗣️ Language
English is widely spoken. Locals also speak Maltese — a few words go a long way.
💳 Currency
Malta uses the Euro (€). Cards are widely accepted.
